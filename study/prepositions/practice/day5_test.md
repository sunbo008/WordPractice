### Part 2：改错题（20 分，每题 2 分）

**找出并改正句子中的介词错误（每句 1-2 个错误）**

1. I go to school in Monday morning at bus.

2. He was born at 2010 in May 5th.

3. Put the book in the shelf on the corner.

4. We arrived at Beijing in 8 pm at Friday.

5. The cat is jumping in the sofa now.

6. I have studied English since three years.

7. Walk across the tunnel and turn in the left.

8. Get in the bus in the next stop.

9. The picture is in the wall above the sofa.

10. We stayed there during two weeks in the summer.

<details>
<summary>点击查看答案</summary>

1. **in** Monday → **on** Monday; **at** bus → **by** bus

2. **at** 2010 → **in** 2010; **in** May 5th → **on** May 5th

3. **in** the shelf → **on** the shelf; **on** the corner → **in** the corner

4. **at** Beijing → **in** Beijing; **in** 8 pm → **at** 8 pm; **at** Friday → **on** Friday

5. **in** the sofa → **onto** the sofa

6. **since** three years → **for** three years

7. **across** the tunnel → **through** the tunnel; **in** the left → **to/on** the left

8. **in** the bus → **on** the bus; **in** the next stop → **at** the next stop

9. **in** the wall → **on** the wall

10. **during** two weeks → **for** two weeks

**得分标准**：

- 18-20 分：优秀！
- 14-17 分：良好
- 12-13 分：及格
- 11 分以下：需要复习
</details>

### Part 4：翻译题（15 分，每题 3 分）

**将下列句子翻译成英文，注意介词的正确使用**

1. 我每天早上 7 点起床，8 点到学校。

2. 我的生日是 10 月 15 日，我出生在 2010 年。

3. 书在桌子上，桌子在房间的角落里。

4. 我从家走到公园，然后穿过公园去学校。

5. 我自从 2020 年起就住在这里，已经 5 年了。

<details>
<summary>点击查看参考答案</summary>

1. I get up **at** 7:00 **in** the morning every day, and arrive **at** school **at** 8:00.
   或: I get up **at** 7 every morning and get **to** school **at** 8.

2. My birthday is **on** October 15th, and I was born **in** 2010.

3. The book is **on** the desk/table, and the desk/table is **in** the corner **of** the room.

4. I walk **from** home **to** the park, and then go **through** the park **to** school.

5. I have lived here **since** 2020, **for** 5 years.
   或: I have lived here **for** 5 years **since** 2020.

**评分标准**（每题 3 分）：

- 所有介词都正确：3 分
- 1 个介词错误：2 分
- 2 个介词错误：1 分
- 3 个或以上错误：0 分
</details>

### Part 5：写作题（15 分）

**根据提示写一篇短文，描述你昨天的活动。要求：**

- 80-100 词
- 使用至少 12 个不同的介词
- 包括时间、地点、方向介词
- 使用至少 3 个介词短语动词

**提示内容：**

- 几点起床
- 去了哪里
- 做了什么
- 和谁一起
- 几点回家
- 晚上做什么

<details>
<summary>点击查看参考范文及评分标准</summary>

**参考范文：**

Yesterday was a wonderful day. I got up **at** 7:00 **in** the morning. **After** breakfast, I went **to** the park **with** my friends. We played basketball **in** the park **for** two hours.

**At** noon, we had lunch **at** a restaurant **near** the park. The restaurant is **on** Main Street, **between** a bookstore and a supermarket.

**In** the afternoon, we walked **through** the city center and went **to** the cinema. We watched a movie **on** the second floor. The cinema is very big and modern.

I went back home **at** 5 pm. **In** the evening, I did my homework **for** one hour. Then I looked **up** some new words **in** my dictionary. I am interested **in** learning English. I went **to** bed **at** 10 pm.

(使用介词：at, in, after, to, with, for, near, on, between, through, up, 共 12 个以上)
(介词短语动词：went to, looked up, interested in, went to bed)

**评分标准**（15 分）：

- **内容完整性**（5 分）：

  - 包含所有提示内容：5 分
  - 遗漏 1-2 项：3-4 分
  - 遗漏 3 项以上：0-2 分

- **介词使用**（7 分）：

  - 使用 12 个以上不同介词，全部正确：7 分
  - 使用 12 个以上，有 1-2 个错误：5-6 分
  - 使用 10-11 个，大部分正确：3-4 分
  - 使用少于 10 个或错误较多：0-2 分

- **介词短语动词**（3 分）：
  - 使用 3 个以上，全部正确：3 分
  - 使用 2 个，正确：2 分
  - 使用 1 个：1 分
  - 未使用或全错：0 分
  </details>
